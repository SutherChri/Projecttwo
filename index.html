Run'n'Draw



A motivational running application that challenges you to run, gain points and afterwards create an artistic masterpiece to commemorate your accomplishments.




Schedule yourself to go for a run and gain points.

Complete challenges to gain even more points than usual.



After a run, get creative with heaps of brush variants to choose from, and make a drawing of any kind to commemorate your achievements.



Use points to unlock different brush shapes, different font styles, and even make your text/brush glow!
